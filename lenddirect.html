LendDirect: Research Intern

Research Intern (Lead or Team Member)

  • LendDirect
  • London, UK (Remote)
  • Part-time

Alongside our partnership with LendDirect, WDSS is responsible (exclusively) for the recruitment of their paid internships. We will be hosting a talk on Thursday, 27th August at 6pm to learn more about the company and the roles on offer. You can watch this live or on playback at this link. Further details on the job roles such as desired candidate attributes and application details will be added to this page following the talk.

The Roles

LendDirect is looking for multiple University of Warwick students to form a research team. This will be formed of a team lead and other team members.
  • A successful applicant will be a self-motivated and proactive student with an interest/background in data science (and ideally the UK property market).
  • The lead will have past leadership and data science project experience, whereas a team member only needs strong team-working skills.
  • Your initial role will be as an intern (start-date ASAP) within LendDirect, but with the strong potential for part-time employment afterwards.
  • You will be disrupting the traditional approach to property price movements by utilising both Land Registry and property listing prices, alongside house features and local area information, to create both hyper-local and regional house price indices.
  • You will combine existing methodologies with new approaches and new data to glean insights that are more meaningful, and which better reflect the “real world” compared to existing indices.
  • Remuneration for work will be in the form of multiple research grants, paid at the completion of various milestones.

About the Company

  • LendDirect is a FinTech business founded by a former Goldman Sachs partner.
  • Since commencing operations, it has successfully built out its first lending business and is now in the process of exploring opportunities in several other lending segments.
  • The business is already solidly profitable. They are seeking to monetise their proprietary software by licensing it to other enterprises. One such offshoot is its property data analytics which has been spun off into a separate subsidiary, Resipedia, which encompasses the project in which you would be involved.
  • Resipedia aims to rapidly become the leading provider of data analytics for UK residential property and to then expand internationally.

How to Apply

    This role is only open to University of Warwick students.
  • Use the application button on this page or email recruitment@warwickdatascience.com.
  • The body of your application should specify whether you prefer a position as team lead or member (or no preference), and contain answers to the following questions:
    • Why in particular LendDirect? What interests you about the UK property market?
    • Do you have an example of a data science-related project you've worked on (ideally involving Python)? How did you challenge and improve on expectations?
    • (Lead only) When have you organised an event and/or managed a team? How did you demonstrate leadership skills?
    • (Team member only) When have you demonstrated strong team-working skills?
  • You are welcome to include any other details you believe are relevant to the application in the email body.
  • Please attach a CV and any links to relevant experience.
  • We will respond to your application shortly after the deadline, providing feedback if you do not proceed.
  • Candidates that make it through to the final stage will be asked to record a one minute video CV and take part in a data scientific coding exercise.
