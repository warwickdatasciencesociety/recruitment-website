WDSS: Marketing Officer

Marketing Officer

  • WDSS
  • Coventry, UK (Remote)
  • Part-time

WDSS is searching for a marketing officer to support our growth, allowing us to reach out to a wide range of students about the importance of data science.

Role Responsibilities

  • Manage our social media platforms (Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter), promoting our offerings and events.
  • Produce promotional material with the support of our Graphic Designer.
  • Drive engagement and a sense of community within the society.

Candidate Attributes

  • Experience with managing a social media account.
  • Strong language and promotional skills.
  • Track record for an attention to detail and high expectations of outputs.
  • Data science knowledge in any form is not required, but a passion for the role certainly is.

How to Apply

  • Use the application button on this page or email recruitment@wdss.io.
  • The body of your application should contain one short paragraph for each of the following points:
    • Why you wish to take up the role
    • How you are qualified for the role
    • What your interest is in WDSS
  • Please attach a CV and any links to relevant experience.
  • We will promptly get in touch to discuss the role further.
