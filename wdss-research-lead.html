WDSS: Research Lead

Research Lead

  • WDSS
  • Coventry, UK (Remote)
  • Part-time

WDSS is searching for an Research Lead to both teach select courses and manage the development and running of others.

Role Responsibilities

  • Manage the society research blog, building teams of students to work on interdisciplinary projects
  • Arrange support for students undertaking research projects with the society.
  • Lead the weekly reading group, the Papers and Code club.
  • Manage and organize the ML competition team.

Candidate Attributes

  • Have a broad knowledge of data science with an interest in its interdisciplinary applications
  • A curiosity of data science and an eagerness to explore it with great rigour across disciplines.
  • Knowledge of Python and R will be expected with other languages being a bonus.
  • Strong time-management, leadership and communication skills.

How to Apply

  • Use the application button on this page or email recruitment@warwickdatascience.com.
  • The body of your application should contain one short paragraph for each of the following points:
    • Why you wish to take up the role
    • How you are qualified for the role
    • What your interest is in WDSS
  • Please attach a CV and a link to your GitHub profile (and/or any other relevant sources).
  • We will promptly get in touch to discuss the role further.
